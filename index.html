About Our Services

Persian Translate & Thrive provides professional Farsi/Dari interpretation and translation services. We specialize in Over-the-Phone Interpretation (OPI), Video Remote Interpretation (VRI), and document translation between English and Persian languages.

Our team consists of native-speaking linguists with expertise in medical, legal, business, and technical terminology. We serve healthcare providers, legal professionals, businesses, and individuals who need accurate communication across these languages.

With certifications in specialized fields and a deep understanding of cultural nuances, we ensure your messages maintain their intended meaning while respecting cultural contexts.

Document Translation

We provide certified and notarized translations for all types of documents with guaranteed accuracy and confidentiality.

Document Types We Translate:

  • Legal Documents (contracts, court papers, affidavits)
  • Medical Records (reports, prescriptions, clinical trials)
  • Business Materials (presentations, marketing content)
  • Personal Documents (birth certificates, marriage licenses)
  • Academic Transcripts and Diplomas
  • Technical Manuals and Patents

Our Process:

  1. Submit your documents through our secure portal
  2. Receive a free quote and timeline
  3. Our certified translators work on your project
  4. Quality assurance by second linguist
  5. Delivery of final translated documents
